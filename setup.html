Domain Setup Guide
1 Add these DNS records

Log in to your domain registrar and add these 2 records. This connects your domain to the GIFTELIX website.

TypeNameValue
A @ 76.76.21.21
CNAME www cname.vercel-dns.com

A record = points your main domain (giftelix.com) to the server.
CNAME = points www.giftelix.com to the same place.
TTL: leave as default or set to 300 (5 min).

2 Where to add DNS records

Click your domain registrar below to open their DNS settings page:

Cloudflare Namecheap GoDaddy Google Domains Hostinger

Don't see yours? Search "[your registrar] add DNS record" on Google.

3 Check if DNS is working

After adding the records, enter your domain below to check. DNS can take 5 min to 48 hours to propagate.
